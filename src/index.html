Ruter Reis Discount Calculator

Find out what discount you can get on Oslo public transport tickets

How Reis Discounts Work:

  • First 4 tickets: 0% discount
  • Tickets 5-9: 5% discount
  • Tickets 10-14: 10% discount
  • Tickets 15-19: 15% discount
  • And so on... up to 40% maximum discount

* Discounts are calculated based on tickets purchased in the last 30 days

* Period ticket prices are approximate and may vary
